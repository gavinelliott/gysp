I do not have any of these

You can send the original or a copy of 2 of the following documents:

  • passport
  • driving licence
  • certificate of naturalisation or citizenship
  • medical card
  • school certificate

    • Certified copies must be dated and signed by 1 of the following people confirming that it's a true copy of the original:

    • bank official (authorised to sign documents)
    • government official (authorised to sign documents)
    • doctor or surgeon (registered in the country you are applying from)
    • solicitor or barrister (authorised to practice in the country you are applying from)
    • civil servant
    • minister of religion
    • chartered accountant

    Include your National Insurance number and send your document to:
