Research notes

Most of the users would have failed the Verify journey due to a lack of confidence online and uncertainty with the role of private companies in the journey. Participants were confused as to what information the companies held on them and what they were providing as well as why this process could not be conducted by the government. There was additional concern about this journey in the context of the invitation letter question and several users mentioned they might call up to request an invitation. Other issues included the need for clarification of certain points, such as around length of stay in hospital and marital status, while if they were entering an email they would expect a confirmation that the process was completed which would offer extra reassurance that everything had been done correctly and their claim had been received.
