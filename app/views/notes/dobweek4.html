Location in service returned to the same as week two in Leeds Library. Content changed regarding National Insurance number to see whether or not users understand they need to include this when sending documents. Removed the print and save option to reduce page length.

Darlington – big success rate; in service information helped and all participants completed the journey and sent relevant documents. Gateshead – the small content change to inform users of time scale increased expectations and caused confusion. Some users did not confirm they would send their NINO with the document.
