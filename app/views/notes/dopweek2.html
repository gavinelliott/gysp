Research notes

Some users had considered deferring their pension though it was clear that few of them would have made this decision at this point in the service. For the most part they had made the decision not to defer prior to reaching this point and would have known to come back later if they had wished to do so. As we have found before most users simply want their pension as soon as possible, and both those who had considered but dismissed deferring and those who had never heard of it, were confused as to why they would need to address a date of claim question in service. Some of these users were uncomfortable with providing a telephone number as they would be unwilling to discuss their finances over the phone without prior verification of the callers identity. In addition contact via email would support the confirmation of application as well as reassuring users who they are dealing with. Other issue included the confusion of the Government Gateway service with GOV.UK Verify - potentially leading to confusion - and the clarity around some content around date of claim.
