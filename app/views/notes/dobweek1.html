Feature
Requesting documents

Date of birth assertion

Week 1 of research in North Sheilds library.

First iteration

Users asked to send documents at the beginning of the service immediately if they have not provided a date that matches our records. They can carry on in the service however their claim is not complete until they have sent documents to prove their date of birth. They were given a list of documents in this iteration, one from one list or two from another.

Research objectives

To gather insight into users experience, understanding, expectations and behaviour in regards to providing their DoB and encountering the potential outcome of sending documents, and being faced with failure pages.
