International Non Verified DOB

The international journey with the document request feature for non-verified users who do not self assert their date of birth correctly when asked in service. Small changes have been made from the GB document request, such as certified copies being accepted as well as options other than birth certificates. SMEs advised us that less international users have their original birth certificates.
