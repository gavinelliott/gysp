Get your State Pension

Use this service to get your State Pension online.

You'll need:

  • the date of your most recent marriage, civil partnership or divorce
  • dates of any time spent living or working abroad
  • your personal or joint bank or building society details

If you have received a letter about getting your State Pension, you can use the invitation code from that.

If you have not received an invitation letter and you are within 4 months of reaching your State Pension age, you can still use the online service.

Other ways to get your State Pension

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

There's a different way to get your State Pension if you live in Northern Ireland, or if you live outside of the UK and have not received an invitation letter.

The online service is the quickest way to get your State Pension, but you can also apply by phone or print and post a form.
