How to get your State Pension


If you have not reached your State Pension date yet

You should receive an invitation letter no later than 2 months before your State Pension date. If you do not receive a letter by this time, you can sign in with your GOV.UK Verify account.

If you have reached your State Pension date

You need to call us to get your State Pension. You will need your National Insurance number and to confirm some personal details.
