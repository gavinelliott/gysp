What happens next

If more information's needed someone will contact you in the next 5 days.

Otherwise you'll get a letter in the next 10 days confirming when your pension will start and how much you'll get.

If your circumstances change

You must tell the Department for Work and Pensions of changes to your circumstances e.g. your address, spouse or partner, or bank account details.

Telephone: 0845 60 60 265

If you don’t report changes in reasonable time you could be paid too much pension by mistake. You’ll have to repay this money.

If you refuse to repay when asked then a court can allow money to be taken directly from your pension. This may mean you get less pension or your pension is stopped until the money’s repaid.
