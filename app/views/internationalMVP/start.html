Get your State Pension

*CHANGES*

Use this service to get your State Pension.

You'll need:

  • the date of your most recent marriage, civil partnership or divorce
  • dates of time spent living or working abroad
  • your personal or joint bank or building society details

If you don’t want to get your State Pension now, you don’t have to do anything. You may get more State Pension if you put off claiming.

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg)


Start now

Before you start

You will receive a letter inviting you to get your State Pension up to 2 months before your State Pension age.

You can also use the service if you are over State Pension age or have put off claiming your State Pension.

If you haven't received an invitation letter to get your State Pension, you can still claim online.

If you live in Northern Ireland, you’ll need to get your State Pension with NI Direct.

There's a different way to claim if you live overseas.

You can also claim by phone or by post.
