Get your State Pension

You’ll receive a letter before you reach State Pension age. The letter will contain the invite code you’ll need to use this service.

Use this service when you're ready to get your state pension.

You will need your:

  • your invite code (on your Get your State Pension letter)
  • your National Insurance number
  • your partner's details, including the date you were married or became civil partners
  • dates of any previous marriages or civil partnerships
  • your bank details

If you don't want your State Pension straight away

If you don't want to get your State Pension, then you don't have to do anything now.

You won't get any State Pension until you tell us you want it.

You may get extra money if you delay taking your State Pension.
